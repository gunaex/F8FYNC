# F8SYNC V8 Physical Product Guide

## Scope

Physical products are future-facing in V8. The MVP may model data and mock proof workflows, but real fulfillment must remain disabled unless explicitly enabled and reviewed.

## Product Types

- personalized single card
- collector single card
- curated deck
- random pack
- box set
- gift bundle

## Print Requirements

- 300 DPI minimum target.
- Finished size, bleed, and safe area must be configurable.
- Color profile metadata is required.
- Front and back template versions are required.
- Print-safe typography and crop-safe areas are required.
- Proof and sample approval are required before production use.

## Personalized Card Privacy

Personalized print products may include:

- user-chosen display name
- primary archetype
- approved short meaning
- symbolic seal
- non-sensitive elemental summary
- edition
- optional authenticated QR code

They must not expose by default:

- full birth date
- full birth time
- exact birth location
- member ID
- email
- private analysis text
- hidden premium result

## Fulfillment Boundary

- Real fulfillment defaults disabled.
- User must preview and consent before order creation.
- No print job may be created from unreviewed AI art.
- Mock commerce and mock fulfillment providers are acceptable for MVP.

# F8SYNC V8 Art Direction Bible

## Direction

Premium Southeast Asian celestial editorial fantasy plus modern wellness product clarity plus original collectible-card composition.

## Mood

- Calm
- Private
- Premium
- Trustworthy
- Reflective
- Collectible

## Color System

Recommended accent families:

- Dark slate
- Ivory
- Muted gold
- Jade
- Vermilion
- Bronze
- Deep blue

Avoid one-note palettes and excessive gold across every surface.

## Character and Archetype Rules

- Original silhouettes only.
- No third-party character resemblance.
- No automatic assignment of real religious figures.
- Avoid hypersexualized presentation.
- Avoid excessive violence or fear imagery.
- Characters should read clearly at thumbnail size.

## Symbol Rules

- Symbols are product-original or approved methodology symbols.
- No fake sacred text presented as real writing.
- No symbol may be described as guaranteed protection.
- Cultural-review-required motifs must remain draft until approved.

## Composition Rules

- Generate art without final text.
- Typography, icons, collector numbers, and accessibility labels are rendered by the card composition engine.
- Maintain text-safe areas and crop-safe margins for mobile, share previews, and print.
- Provide reduced-motion alternatives for foil, glow, parallax, and reveal effects.

## Export Targets

- Web card art.
- Thumbnail.
- Hero banner.
- Print card art.
- Print masters must target 300 DPI minimum with bleed and safe areas.

## Prohibited Visual Cliches

- Generic AI fantasy noise.
- Overloaded ornamental frames that obscure meaning.
- Tarot cliches as the primary product language.
- Horror or doom framing for ordinary caution content.
- Copyrighted franchise resemblance.
- Unreviewed religious worship imagery.

## Review Checklists

AI artifact checklist:

- Hands, limbs, eyes, symbols, and borders are coherent.
- No unreadable generated text.
- No accidental logos.
- No personal data in artwork.

Cultural review checklist:

- Motifs are appropriate for product use.
- No unsupported claims of blessing, protection, curse, or guaranteed outcome.
- Thai and Southeast Asian-inspired geometry remains respectful and original.

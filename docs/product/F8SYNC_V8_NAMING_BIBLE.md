| Timing experience | F8SYNC Timing Calendar | Calendar and event surface. |

## Naming Rules

- Names must be short enough for mobile and card headers.
- Names must not imply guaranteed power, protection, success, wealth, safety, health, or relationships.
- Names must not insult, rank, or label users as better/worse.
- Religious or culturally sensitive titles require review before approval.
- Final production card names must be registry-approved, not generated dynamically by AI.
- Thai copy is primary; English and Simplified Chinese must follow the same key structure.
